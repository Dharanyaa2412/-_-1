YOUR TIME WILL COME

A Story Of Second Chances In Life When We Fall Hard.

Love can break the strongest of men and they are the ones to fall hard. But after the fall, will they be able to bounce back and start afresh?

About Author

A university topper twice academically, Saranya Umakanthan is a software engineer by profession. With her novel, " One Day, Life Will Change" she has ventured into the world of inspiring romantic stories. With her passion for writing, she wishes to leave an imprint in people' s hearts, weaving beautiful stories with her words. A romantic by nature, Saranya loves gazing at the night sky while enjoying a cup of coffee. Nothing brings her more contentmet than seeing a reader enjoy her book. The fragrance and texture of paperbacks inspire her and she hangs out at bookstores frequently.
